Why partner with us?

You deserve to give with confidence, so we’ll always stand by our first promise: 100% of all public donations fund clean water. But we don’t stop there. From best-in-class reporting to first-of-its-kind technology, “good enough” is never good enough for us. These organizations agree.

Partnering for progress

Our work is aligned with nine of the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). We take data collection, transparency, and sustainability seriously, which means you’ll be uniquely positioned to prioritize your Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) goals.

In good company

Our partnerships are as unique as the 1,000+ companies in our community. With three different giving levels and endless ways to contribute, it’s never been easier to make generosity on‑brand.

Let’s build a better world together

More people with clean water means more kids who have time for school. More parents with the ability to start and grow a business. More community leaders who know the future is so, so bright. 

We wake up every day determined to make progress on our first and forever goal: Ending the global water crisis. These are the core values that guide and define our mission.
